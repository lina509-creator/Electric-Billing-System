Payment Methods

At ElectronPay, we offer a variety of payment methods to ensure convenience for our users:

  • Credit Card
  • Debit Card
  • Net Banking
  • UPI

How to Use ElectronPay for Payments

Follow these steps to use ElectronPay for your electricity bill payments:

  1. Login to your ElectronPay account.
  2. Navigate to the 'Pay Bills' section.
  3. Enter the required details such as electricity usage and customer name.
  4. Select your preferred payment method.
  5. Complete the payment using your chosen method.
  6. Receive a confirmation of your payment.
